Founders

Sudhakar N

Sudhakar is a serial entrepreneur who started his entrepreneur journey with Eone, an ed-tech startup in his final years of college. Being a Hyderabadi, Sudhakar found a dearth of good hyderabadi biryani in Mumbai and decided to launch his own food tech start-up in the QSR category. His chain of outlets expanded to Mumbai and Pune. He later moved on to join Supr Daily where he headed operations for entire Mumbai. As an operations lead, he got hands-on experience in leading large scale operations, execution and streamlining of supply chain and logistics for 60,000 orders daily.

With his experience in handling large scale operations, coupled with data that identifies the huge market opportunity in the FnV sector, Sudhakar launched FnV Farms. FnV Farms delivers 100% fresh fruits and vegetables directly from the farms to the customer's doorstep.
Sudhakar is a Btech from IIT Bombay and loves Indian movies. He is a complete foodie who lives only two dreams- Good food and FnV Farms.

Jamal Farheen

Jamal hails from the city of Joy, Kolkata. She began her career as a corporate communications professional but her love for marketing gradually led her towards marketing communications. She has worked with companies such as Mahindra and Mahindra, Unilever, Abbott where she honed her skills as a marketing professional. Her last stint was with Kimberly-Clark where he led the marketing communication profile for K-C India.
She has a very tactile approach towards the marketing strategy for FnV Farms and will lead vertical and a Co-Founder and CMO.
Jamal is a proud cat mom. Being an excellent cook, she treats cooking and hosting culinary get- together as a stressbuster. She is a diehard foodie who loves travelling to explore new cuisine, indigenous recipes, food ingredients and culture. She plans to publish a book about her culinary explorations soon.
Jamal holds a postgraduate degree from Indian Institute of Mass Communication and an Ex. MBA from IIM Calcutta

Budhaditya Mukherjee

Budhaditya is an engineer by profession but an entrepreneur by choice. Fondly known as Budha, he began his career with Zenopsys as a Strategy Head. Thereafter, Budha joined Cardekho where he led business expansion and went on to conquer more than 20 cities as part of his expansion portfolio. His association with startups right from the beginning of his career fuelled his passion to embark on the path of entrepreneurship.
As a Co-Founder and Expansions lead at FnV Farms, Budha plans to use his business expansion skills to expand to more than 20 Tier I and II cities across India.
Budha has a humorous take on life and can add his touch of humor to even the most mundane things in life. He is an outstanding driver and very passionate about food. He loves good food and will not hesitate to drive to far away places just to explore a good menu.
Budha holds an MBA from IBS, Hyderabad and is an engineer from KLE University, Hubli.

Suhaas Vaddi

Suhaas began his career working with Ed-tech and Core startups where he contributed as a lead product design engineer. He then joined VEFES, a renewable energy start-up where he headed the design and data department. His product design on developing energy from renewables sources has been submitted for patents.

As a Co-Founder at FnV Farms, he will be the lead data scientist and head the strategic planning.

Suhaas is a versatile musician who can play drums, guitar and piano. He is passionately in love with football and is a knowledge reservoir of every team, match and player when it comes to football. His second love is good food. Suhaas is holds a B'tech from IIT Bombay.
